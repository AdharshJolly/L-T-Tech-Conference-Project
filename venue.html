Venue Information

Experience TechSphere 2025 at Bangalore's premier convention center

Bangalore International Convention Centre

📍 Address

26/1, Dr. Rajkumar Road,
Malleshwaram, Bangalore - 560055
Karnataka, India

A world-class facility spanning over 50,000 square feet, the Bangalore International Convention Centre offers state-of-the-art technology and modern amenities. The venue features multiple auditoriums, exhibition halls, and networking spaces designed to accommodate large-scale technology conferences.

🏢 Venue Features

  • ✓ 5,000-seat main auditorium with advanced AV systems
  • ✓ 10 breakout rooms for workshops and sessions
  • ✓ 20,000 sq ft exhibition hall
  • ✓ High-speed WiFi throughout the venue
  • ✓ Live streaming capabilities
  • ✓ Accessibility features for all attendees

Getting There

✈️

From Airport

Kempegowda International Airport

  • 🚗 Taxi/Uber: 45-60 minutes (₹800-1200)
  • 🚌 Airport Bus: BMTC Vayu Vajra (₹250)
  • 🚇 Metro + Bus: Take Purple Line to Majestic, then bus
🚇

By Metro

Nearest Station: Majestic

  • 🚇 Take Green Line to Majestic Station
  • 🚌 Bus #201, #226 to Dr. Rajkumar Road
  • 🚶‍♂️ 5-minute walk from bus stop
🚗

By Car

Driving Directions

  • 🛣️ Take Outer Ring Road to Dr. Rajkumar Road
  • 🅿️ Ample parking available on-site
  • ⚡ EV charging stations available
