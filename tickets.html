Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket at any time before the event. You'll only pay the difference in price.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund available until 30 days before the event. 50% refund until 14 days before. No refunds after that.

Are recordings available?

Yes! All sessions are recorded. Standard tickets get 90-day access, Premium gets lifetime access.

Is there a dress code?

Business casual is recommended. Comfortable shoes are a must as you'll be walking between sessions.
